Payment Gateway

A payment gateway is a digital service that securely processes and authorizes transactions for online and in-store purchases. Acting as an intermediary between the customer and merchant, it ensures that payment details like credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and other methods are securely transmitted. Payment gateways enable businesses to accept various payment options, including cards, e-wallets, and even cryptocurrencies, while ensuring secure and efficient transactions.

Fake News Detection

Fake news detection combines text analysis, machine learning, social media monitoring, and fact-checking to identify and reduce misinformation. It involves assessing the credibility of news, identifying false patterns, and verifying claims with trusted sources. As digital content increases, AI and advanced algorithms are key to improving accuracy and speed. These tools help detect fake news across platforms. This process ensures the timely mitigation of misleading information. The use of AI is vital for combating the growing spread of fake news.
